### First Comment (第一条评论，在产品上线后立即发布)

这是最重要的部分！第一条评论会置顶，是你讲故事的机会。

```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm excited to launch AI Divination today! 🎴✨

## 🌟 The Story

I built this because I've always been fascinated by divination practices—tarot, palm reading, dream interpretation—but found most online tools either too commercial or lacking depth. I wanted to create something that's:

✅ Completely free (no paywalls, no signup)
✅ Privacy-focused (no data collection)
✅ Multilingual (EN/ZH/FR/JA)
✅ Shareable (perfect for social media)
✅ Thoughtful (AI-generated literary content, not generic horoscopes)

## 🎯 What Makes It Different?

Unlike traditional divination apps, AI Divination focuses on:
- **Literary quality**: The Daily Reading feature generates original poetry and prose, not recycled fortune cookie messages
- **Cultural authenticity**: Supports Chinese lunar calendar, Buddhist fortune sticks, and respects cultural traditions
- **No dark patterns**: No email capture, no notifications, no upsells—just pure experience

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

Built with Next.js 15, powered by AI (GPT), uses lunar-javascript for accurate Chinese calendar calculations, and deployed on Vercel.

## 🙏 What I'd Love Feedback On

1. Which feature do you find most interesting?
2. Any cultural practices you'd like to see added?
3. How can I improve the AI interpretations?

Try it out and let me know what you think! Your feedback will help shape the future of this project. 🚀

Link: https://aidivination.net
```

## 📅 发布时间建议

### 最佳发布时间
- **日期：** 周二、周三、或周四（避开周一和周五）
- **时间：** 美国太平洋时间 00:01 AM（北京时间下午4:01）
- **为什么：** Product Hunt 每天 00:01 AM PST 重置排行榜，早发布可以积累更多时间的投票

### 发布日历
1. 提前3-5天准备所有素材
2. 提前2天提交审核（通常24小时内审核）
3. 选择发布日期
4. 发布当天全天在线回复评论
